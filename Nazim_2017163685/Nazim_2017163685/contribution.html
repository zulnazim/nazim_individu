Tunku Abdul Rahman's Contributions

During Tunku Abdul Rahman's tenure as Malaysia's prime minister, he contributed a lot to the country in the hope that Malaysia would achieve whatever was considered impossible to achieve. As a result, we have now achieved many great successes such as Malaysians are united regardless of race and ethnicity

Other Tunku Abdul Rahman's contributions

Tunku Abdul Rahman has inspired Al-Quran competitions at the state and international levels which have made Malaysia famous. Back home, Tunku continued to be active in the field of propaganda to help the non-Malays to Islam. Tunku leads PERKIM or the Malaysian Islamic Welfare Organization.

He is the founder of the Merdeka Football Festival which is so popular and welcomed by other countries in Asia. He was once the President of the Football Association of Malaysia and the President of the Asian Football Confederation. Before independence was President of the Football Association of Indonesia and President of the Football Association of Selangor.

Unite the races while maintaining the integrity of the Malays and the Malaysian Chinese Association (Malayan Chinese Association). The UMNO-MCA "Alliance" has given victory to both. On the 1955 elections the Alliance is accompanied by the MIC (Malaysian Indian Congress) as representing the Indians. With this Alliance organization all the important races are represented in one of the largest political bodies moving towards independence.
